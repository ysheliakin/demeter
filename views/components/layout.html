Connecting communities one donation at a time.

Reduce Food Waste

In the United States, an estimated 30-40% of the food supply is wasted each year. That equates to approximately 108 billion pounds of food thrown away, contributing to economic losses of over $161 billion.

Fight Food Insecurity

At the same time, food insecurity touches millions of lives across the country. Over 34 million people, including 9 million children, face food insecurity, lacking consistent access to enough food for an active, healthy life.

Community Support

At Demeter, we bridge the gap between surplus and need. We are a community-driven platform that connects local businesses with excess food to charitable organizations and individuals in need. By donating through Demeter, you help reduce food waste, conserve resources, and most importantly, provide essential nutrition to those who need it most.

Join Our Effort

Be a part of our mission to turn surplus into support. Donate. Volunteer. Advocate. With every donation, you're not just giving away food; you're giving hope.
